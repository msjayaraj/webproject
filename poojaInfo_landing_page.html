🪔 Daily Pooja Schedule

5:30 AM – Suprabhata Seva

Morning prayer to awaken the deity with Vedic chants and music.

7:00 AM – Abhishekam

Holy bath with milk, honey, sandalwood paste, and sacred water.

9:00 AM – Archana

Offering flowers and chanting.

12:00 PM – Madhyana Pooja

Noon prayers followed by naivedyam and distribution of prasadam.

6:30 PM – Sandhya Aarti

Evening worship with lamps and devotional songs.

8:00 PM – Shayana Seva

Night prayers before the temple closes for the day.

🌼 Special Poojas & Festivals

  • Vinayaka Chathurthy: Grand celebration with bhajans, alankara, and prasadam.
  • Rama Navami: Rama Pattabhisheka rituals and special darshan.
  • Amavasya Abhishekam: Monthly early morning abhishekam and homa.
  • Sankashti Chaturthi: Prayers and fasting dedicated to Lord Ganesha.
