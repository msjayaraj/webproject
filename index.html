Welcome to Kuttiyankavu Temple

The Legend of Kuttiyankavu: A Tale of Devotion and Divine Intervention

In the tranquil village of Minaloor, near Athani in Thrissur district, Kerala, stands the revered Sree Kuttiyankavu Bhagavathi Temple. Nestled amidst lush paddy fields and shaded by a venerable banyan tree, this temple is a beacon of faith and tradition. Dedicated to Goddess Bhagavathi, with Lord Ayyappa as the Upa Devata, the temple is a spiritual haven for devotees seeking solace and blessings.
